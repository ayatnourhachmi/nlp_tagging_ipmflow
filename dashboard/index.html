What is F1?

F1 scores how well each model assigns the correct IPM Flow labels (Objective, Domain, Impact, Origin) to business pitches. It balances precision (avoiding wrong tags) and recall (finding the right tags). Values range from 0 to 1 — higher is better. Avg F1 is the mean across all four taxonomy dimensions on a held-out test set. Per-dimension scores use weighted F1, so frequent labels do not dominate rare ones.
